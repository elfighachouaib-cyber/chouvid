About Us
We believe anyone
can create great video

ChouVid was built by Chouaib — a developer who was frustrated with how complicated and expensive video creation tools were. Most video generators require expensive subscriptions, a powerful PC, or hours of editing skills.

So we built ChouVid: a fully AI-powered video generator that runs entirely in your browser. No installs. No editing. Just describe your idea and get a real downloadable video in under a minute.

We use Mistral AI for scripts, Pexels for footage, Pixabay for music, and your browser's built-in speech engine for narration. Everything assembled automatically — for free.

How To Use
Create your first video
in 5 simple steps

No editing skills needed. No downloads. Just your browser and an idea.

1

Sign in with Google

Click "Get Started" and sign in with your Google account. It takes 10 seconds and requires no credit card. You get 1 free video immediately.

💡 Free plan — no card needed
2

Write your video idea

In the dashboard, type what you want your video to be about. It can be as simple as "a motivational video for students who are stressed before exams" or "a promotional video for a coffee shop".

💡 One sentence is enough — AI handles the rest
3

Pick a video style

Choose from Motivational, Educational, Promotional, Storytelling, or Tutorial. This helps the AI write the right tone and structure for your video.

💡 You can always generate again with a different style
4

Hit Generate and wait ~30 seconds

ChouVid will write your script scene by scene, find matching stock footage, load background music, generate the voiceover, and record everything into a real video — all automatically in your browser.

💡 Keep your browser tab open while generating
5

Download and share your video

Your video is ready as a .webm file. Download it and post directly to TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts or any platform. Free plan includes a small watermark — upgrade to Pro to remove it.

💡 .webm plays on Chrome, Firefox, Edge — use a free converter for .mp4
