How To Use
From text to audio
in 4 simple steps
1

Sign in with Google

Click "Get Started" and sign in. Free, no credit card. You get 1 free audio immediately.

💡 Free plan — 250 characters max
2

Type or paste your text

Enter any text — a social media caption, study notes, a script, a product description. Up to your plan's limit.

💡 Pro plan gets 1,000 characters per audio
3

Pick your language and voice

Choose English or French. Then select from multiple voice styles — calm, upbeat, deep, professional.

💡 Different voices work better for different content
4

Generate and download

Hit Generate. Your audio plays in the browser and you can download it to use anywhere — YouTube, TikTok, podcasts, presentations.

💡 Use Chrome for the best voice quality
